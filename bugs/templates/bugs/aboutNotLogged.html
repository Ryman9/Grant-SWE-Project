About Bug Tracker

Bug tracker allows you to record bugs and other issues within your projects. Now your team of engineers have a fun and easy tool to use for your engineering projects and endeavors! Bug Tracker is easy to use simply follow the following steps 1. In the dashboard you have an overall of all the tickets for your project. An overall of all the tickets assigned to you and their attributes are shown to let you know which tickets need work, when it was created, and the urgency to fix. To create your own ticket, simple click Create ticket and you will be taken to the create ticket page to input your ticket. Here in the create ticket page, enter a title and category for your ticket and describe the problem and its urgency. Once you finished, make sure your ticket is filled out completely and correct and then click submit ticket to submit the ticket to the system. To update any tickets in the system, simply click create update to update a ticket. Here input your name and select the ticket ID you want to update Then provide a description of the update to the ticket. In the case that the problem associated with the ticket is resolved, simply click "Problem has been resolved?" Finally click "Update Ticket" to input the update to the system. In the case that you selected Problem has been resolved?, the ticket will automatically be deleted from the system. In the tickets tab you will have any overview of all the tickets in the system and all of their attributes. If you want to delete a ticket here, click Delete Ticket to go to the delete a ticket page. Here you can input your name and the associated ticket Id you want to delete. Please provide a description as to why you are deleting the ticket and the option to select whether the problem has been resolved. Only administrator users can delete tickets for everyone, so if you cannot delete a ticket assigned to someone else, only the assigned person can. Talk to your administrator about any concerns for deleting a ticket.
